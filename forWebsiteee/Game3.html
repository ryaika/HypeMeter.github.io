Grand Theft Auto 5

Grand Theft Auto V is an open-world action-adventure game that allows players to roam freely throughout a fictional state based in Southern California. It may be played in either third-person or first-person mode, and its world can be explored on foot or by car. The player follows three protagonists through a narrative that is engaged in robbery scenes and several missions that require shooting and driving action.


Release Date: September 17, 2013
Developer: Rockstar North
Platforms: Windows, macOS, Linus, Xbox One, PS4, PS3, Xbox 360
