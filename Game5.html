Animal Crossing: New Horizons

Animal Crossing: New Horizons is a social simulation game played in real time. In New Horizons, the player takes control of a character who moves to a remote island after obtaining a getaway package from Tom Nook. They can play the game in any order they like, and they can develop the island anyway they want. They can gather and craft stuff, personalize the island, and turn it into an anthropomorphic animal community.


Release Date: March 20, 2020
Developer: Nintendo EPD
Platform: Nintendo Switch
