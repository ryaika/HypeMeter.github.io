Call of Duty: Warzone

Call of Duty: Warzone is a free-to-play battle royale video game with Battle Royale and Plunder are the two main modes in the game. Warzone introduces a new in-game cash system that can be used to purchase items at "Buy Stations" across the battlefield. Cash can be exchanged for limited access to players' custom classes "Loadout" drops are an example of where Cash can be exchanged for limited access to players' custom classes, it can also be used to buy things like "killstreaks" and gas masks.


Release Date: March 10, 2020
Developers: Infinity Ward and Raven Software
Platforms: Microsoft Windows PlayStation 4 Xbox One PlayStation 5 Xbox Series X/S
