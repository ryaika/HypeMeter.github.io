League of Legends

League of Legends is a team-based strategy game where two teams of five powerful champions face off to destroy the other's base. Choose from over 140 champions to make epic plays, secure kills, and take down towers as you battle your way to victory. This game has been popular because of its epic strategies and cool heores that makes this game worth the try. Worth mentioning that League of Legends has gained a lot of popularity because of gameplay and game style that it has.
