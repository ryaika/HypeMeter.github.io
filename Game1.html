Minecraft

Minecraft is a "sandbox game" that may be played in either survival or creative mode. Players can break, place, and create various types of blocks or materials. This means that this is an exploration game in which players create their own worlds and experiences by utilizing various types of building blocks or resources as well as their own imagination.


Release Date: November 18, 2011
Developer(s): Mojang Studios, Telltale Games, Xbox Game Studios, 4J Studios, Other Ocean Interactive, Double Eleven
Platforms: Windows, macOS, Linus, Mobile, Xbox One, PS4, PS3, Xbox 360
